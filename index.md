## D.P. Maddalena *Works*

I am a technologist by virtue of location (Silicon Valley) and occasional occupation (former tech-industry writer, current licensed psychotherapist), and an artist in spirit and vocation (making visual and written art from the beginning). I work with pictures, paragraphs, and people. Visit the [category page](http://www.imby.net/categories.html) for a roadmap; track [tags](http://www.imby.net/tags.html) to drill down; or travel through time below.
